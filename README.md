 📊 E-Commerce Sales Dashboard | Power BI

An interactive Power BI dashboard developed to analyze e-commerce sales data and provide actionable business insights through dynamic visualizations and KPIs.

 📌 Project Overview

This project demonstrates how Power BI can be used to transform raw sales data into meaningful business insights. The dashboard consists of two interactive pages that help analyze sales performance, customer behavior, product performance, and regional trends.

---

🚀 Dashboard Features

### 📄 Page 1 – Executive Dashboard
- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Total Orders
- Total Quantity
- Average Discount
- Profit Margin
- Monthly Sales Trend
- Sales by Category
- Profit by Region
- Payment Mode Distribution
- Interactive Slicers

### 📄 Page 2 – Product & Customer Analysis
- Top 10 Products by Sales
- Top 10 Customers by Sales
- Sales by Sub-Category (Treemap)
- Quantity by Category
- City-wise Sales Analysis (Map)
- Interactive Filtering
